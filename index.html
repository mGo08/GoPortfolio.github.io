BSCS - A222

Born in 2004 in Davao City, Philippines.

Currently a second-year Computer Science student at Mapua Malayan Colleges Mindanao. With a strong passion for technology, I am dedicated to advancing my skills and creativity in the field of computer science. I actively engage in various areas of study, including programming, system design, and data structures, with a focus on applying theoretical knowledge to practical projects. I believe in continuous learning and strive to expand my expertise through both academic pursuits and hands-on experiences.

My interest in coding began in 2012 when I discovered Garry’s Mod, a sandbox game that introduced me to the world of programming and digital creation. What started as simple in-game modifications quickly grew into a fascination with scripting, browser extensions, and reverse-engineering game files. This early curiosity evolved into a passion for software development, problem-solving, and understanding how technology can be leveraged to build innovative solutions.

As I progress in my studies, I have explored advanced topics such as the Internet of Things (IoT) and have worked on projects related to coffee shop and car rental services, aiming to develop technology-driven solutions for real-world challenges. Beyond academics, my involvement in esports has helped me cultivate valuable skills in teamwork, strategy, and critical thinking—qualities that translate well into the tech industry.

This portfolio showcases my projects, achievements, and the journey that has shaped me into the developer I am today. I am excited to continue growing, learning, and making meaningful contributions to the field of technology.
